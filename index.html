The Restaurant Collective is an inclusive body formed by leading restaurateurs and industry professionals whose primary goal is to deliver real day-to-day support to its members whilst raising the social profile of the restaurant industry.

The Restaurant Collective is a co-operative organisation, 100% owned by its active membership.

Find out more

What's it all about?

  • Empowering Restaurants

    Using the strength of the collective to negotiate highly competitive booking deals that are equal for all members.

    Ensuring that every restaurant has up-to-date digital content that is easily and freely accessible to 3rd party organisations.

  • Sharing knowledge

    Providing members with free access to expert guides and specialist information authored by its most successful professionals.

    Mentoring opportunities for up-and-coming restaurant professionals, both front and back-of-house plus sponsored scholarship and ‘job exchange’ opportunities within Europe.

  • Providing support

    Creating an online directory of members, with online booking services that are free to both restaurants and diners.

    Creating local online marketing opportunites via easy access partner tools.

  • Giving information & insights

    Offering benchmarking services that members can use to anonymously share their information and gain access to statistical comparisons and industry trends.

    Centralised and searchable job vacancy listing for the industry.

  • Increasing Social Profile

    Helping restaurants to support local community projects and lead social awareness programmes such as healthy eating and food waste redistribution.

  • Building key partnerships

    Securing key partnerships with national industry suppliers and services on behalf of the Collective membership.
